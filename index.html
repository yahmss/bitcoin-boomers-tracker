Bitcoin Booters League Rules

General:

  • League Size: 16 teams.
  • Buy-in: $20 per team, due before the draft.
  • Prizes: The total pot will be converted to Bitcoin.
    • 1st Place: Wins the entire Bitcoin pot minus the amount of Bitcoin equal to the original $20 buy-in.
    • 2nd Place: Wins the Bitcoin amount equivalent to the original $20 buy-in.

Roster:

  • Roster Size: 5 total players.
  • Positions: 1 QB, 1 RB, 1 WR, 1 TE, 1 K.
  • Bench/IR: No bench or IR spots.

Transactions & Free Agents:

  • Lineup Changes: Lock individually at scheduled gametime.
  • Waiver System: Waivers are used.
  • Waiver Order: Resets each week to inverse order of standings.
  • Acquisition Limit: No limit on pickups and drops for the season.
  • Undroppable Players: The ESPN undroppable list is turned off.

Scoring (Head-to-Head Points, Point Per Reception):

  • Field Goals (50-59 yards): 30 points (overriding the standard 4 points)
  • Field Goals (60+ yards): 30 points (overriding the standard 5 points)
  • Receptions: 1 point per reception (This is a PPR league format)
  • All other scoring categories are standard ESPN defaults.

Playoffs:

  • Playoff Teams: 8 teams make the playoffs.
  • Playoff Seeding Tie Breaker: Total Points For.
  • Playoff Matchups: 1 week per playoff round.
